### The meaning of ACID
<ul>
    <li>Atomicity</li>
    <li>Consistency</li>
    <li>Isolation</li>
    <li>Durability</li>
    <ul>
        <li>is the promise that once a transaction has committed successfully, any data it has written will not be forgotten, even if there is a hardware fault or the database crashes.</li>
